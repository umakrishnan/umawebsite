My story

From big tech to public interest.

Today I put that same toolkit to work for the public good: production AI agents for local campaigns, AI tooling for nonprofits and small businesses, and civic organizing across San Mateo County. I'm finishing my Master's in Data Science at UC Berkeley along the way.
